About

Batanes is a stunning archipelago located in the Northern Philippines. The islands are known for their stunning landscapes, rich culture and friendly people. The islands are also a popular destination for travel and vacation because of the beautiful weather, incredible sceneries and friendly locals. And we "Explore Batanes" want to promote this amazing place as a tourist spot! We want to share the wonders of Batanes to the world and let the world know that there is a wonderful place to visit that is waiting to be discovered.
